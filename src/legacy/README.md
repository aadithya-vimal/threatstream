# Archived product surfaces

The pages and supporting modules listed in `MODULE_DISPOSITION.md` are not active ThreatStream product features. Their routes were removed during Phase 1 because they represent the former broad SOC, malware-analysis, threat-intelligence, endpoint, network, and attack-globe product direction.

Source files remain in their original directories temporarily to preserve implementation history and make later, evidence-based repurposing possible. They must not be imported by active routes, advertised in documentation, or treated as completed functionality.

Future migration commits may move or delete these files after reusable components and data-migration requirements are identified.
